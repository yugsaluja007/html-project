Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat a small amount of olive oil and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add pancetta and cook until crispy.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  5. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water, and add to the pan with pancetta.
  6. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  7. If needed, add a bit of reserved pasta water to achieve a creamy consistency.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately with extra cheese if desired.

Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper and cook until fully cooked, then slice.
  2. Chop the romaine lettuce and place it in a large bowl.
  3. Add croutons and Parmesan cheese to the lettuce.
  4. Top with sliced chicken breasts and drizzle with Caesar dressing.
  5. Toss everything together and serve immediately.
